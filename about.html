About Me:

I'm Amad Ihsan, a 23-year-old individual hailing from the culturally rich region of Kurdistan-Iraq, specifically Duhok/Sarsink. In 2023, I proudly graduated from the University of Duhok with a degree in Computer Science. Throughout my academic journey, I have developed a strong foundation in the field, gaining a comprehensive understanding of various computer science principles.

My passion lies in design, where I have honed my skills in both 2D and 3D design, with a particular focus on web design. I find joy and fulfillment in creating visually appealing and functional designs that leave a lasting impression.

I possess expertise in several industry-standard design software, including Adobe Photoshop, Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD and Figma. These tools allow me to bring my creative visions to life and deliver high-quality design solutions.

Additionally, I am well-versed in Blender, a versatile software for 3D modeling and animation. This skill expands my capabilities to create immersive and dynamic visual content.

My dedication to excellence, coupled with a keen eye for detail, motivates me to stay abreast of the latest design trends and technological advancements.

Whether it's crafting compelling visual narratives or developing innovative design solutions, I am dedicated to pushing the boundaries of creativity and making a positive impact in the field of computer science.
